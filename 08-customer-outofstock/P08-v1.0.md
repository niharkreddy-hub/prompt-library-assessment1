## ❗ Problem Being Solved

Out-of-stock situations handled reactively generate significant complaint volume, refund costs, and reputational damage. Proactive empathetic communication significantly reduces churn and support ticket volume. Manual drafting takes 20–30 minutes per customer notification with inconsistent tone and missing resolution options.

**Pain points addressed:**
- Reactive handling increases complaint volume and churn
- Inconsistent tone — some emails too blunt, damaging customer relationships
- Missing resolution options — customers contact support unnecessarily

## 🔄 Version History

### v1.0 — Initial draft
**Date:** 31 March 2026  
**Prompt:** You are a customer service agent. Write an email to a customer telling them their order is delayed because we don't have stock.  
**Output:** Blunt, unsympathetic email with no resolution options, no restock date, and named the supplier. Likely to increase complaints rather than reduce them.  
**Observed effect:** Completely unusable — would damage customer relationships if sent.  
**Lesson learned:** Customer comms need empathetic tone, 3 resolution options, supplier exclusion rule, and restock date. Solution-first framing is essential.
