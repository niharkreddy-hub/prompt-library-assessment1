## ❗ Problem Being Solved

Stock discrepancies cost SwiftFill thousands of dollars monthly in unrecovered shortfalls. Without standardised dispute letters, claims are poorly documented, delayed, or abandoned — resulting in direct financial loss. Manual drafting currently takes 25–35 minutes per incident.

**Pain points addressed:**
- Unrecovered shortfall revenue due to poor dispute documentation
- Inconsistent dispute letters — some too informal, claims not pursued
- No financial quantification — suppliers don't see dollar value of shortfall

## ⚠️ Risks and Limitations

| Risk | Level | Mitigation |
|------|-------|------------|
| No financial value of shortfall — weak claim | High | Fix in v1.1 — add shortfall value in AUD |
| No resolution deadline | High | Fix in v1.1 — add 5 business day resolution request |
| Too informal — won't hold up in dispute | Medium | Fix in v1.1 — add formal tone and contract reference |

**Overall risk rating:** HIGH — v1.0 too informal and incomplete for a financial dispute claim.
