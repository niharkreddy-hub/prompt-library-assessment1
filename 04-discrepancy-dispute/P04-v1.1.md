## 📌 Prompt Text (v1.1)
```
You are a warehouse operations manager at an e-commerce fulfilment company. Draft a formal stock discrepancy notification to [SUPPLIER_NAME] regarding delivery received on [DELIVERY_DATE] under PO [PO_NUMBER]. Our warehouse team recorded receipt of [ACTUAL_UNITS] units against [ORDERED_UNITS] units ordered — a shortfall of [SHORTFALL_UNITS] units valued at approximately [SHORTFALL_VALUE] AUD. Request credit note or replacement shipment within 5 business days. Maintain a factual, professional tone.
```

**Placeholders to fill:**

| Placeholder | Source | Example |
|-------------|--------|---------|
| `[SUPPLIER_NAME]` | Supplier database | AusPack Supplies Pty Ltd |
| `[DELIVERY_DATE]` | Warehouse receiving log | 18 November 2024 |
| `[PO_NUMBER]` | WMS | PO-2024-0847 |
| `[ACTUAL_UNITS]` | Warehouse receiving log | 463 units |
| `[ORDERED_UNITS]` | PO record | 500 units |
| `[SHORTFALL_UNITS]` | Calculated | 37 units |
| `[SHORTFALL_VALUE]` | PO unit price × shortfall | $88.80 AUD |

## 🏢 Intended Workflow or Task

- **Trigger:** Warehouse receiving team identifies shortfall during goods-in verification
- **Actor:** Warehouse manager reviews, attaches receiving docs, and sends within 24 hours
- **Timing:** Same day as goods-in verification completion
- **Next step:** Supplier issues credit note or arranges replacement shipment within 5 days
```
[Goods-in verification] → [Shortfall identified] → [P04 RUNS] → [Manager reviews & sends]
                                                                          ↓
                                                          [Credit note or replacement shipment]
```

---

## ❗ Problem Being Solved

Stock discrepancies cost SwiftFill thousands of dollars monthly in unrecovered shortfalls. Without standardised dispute letters, claims are poorly documented and abandoned — resulting in direct financial loss. Manual drafting takes 25–35 minutes per incident with inconsistent outcomes.

**Pain points addressed:**
- Unrecovered shortfall revenue due to poor dispute documentation
- Inconsistent dispute letters — some too informal, claims not pursued
- No financial quantification — suppliers don't see dollar value at stake

## ⚠️ Risks and Limitations

| Risk | Level | Mitigation |
|------|-------|------------|
| Missing contract clause reference — weaker legal position | Medium | Fix in v1.2 — add clause reference variable |
| No apology exclusion rule — AI may include unnecessary apology | Medium | Fix in v1.2 — add explicit exclusion |
| Minimum threshold not set — sending for trivial shortfalls | Low | Fix in v1.2 — add threshold trigger note |

**Overall risk rating:** MEDIUM — v1.1 much stronger but missing legal grounding.
