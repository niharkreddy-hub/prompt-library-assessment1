## ❗ Problem Being Solved

Monthly supplier reviews are often unstructured and reactive, lacking consistent data-driven framing. Without a standardised summary, procurement managers struggle to compare supplier performance across the portfolio or make evidence-based contract renewal decisions. Manual preparation takes 60–90 minutes per supplier per month.

**Pain points addressed:**
- Unstructured reviews — no consistent agenda or performance framing
- 60–90 min manual preparation per supplier per month
- No health score — contract renewal decisions made without evidence base
