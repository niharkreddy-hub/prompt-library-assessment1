## ❗ Problem Being Solved

Delayed or inconsistent internal communication about incoming shipments leads to unstaffed receiving bays, missed deliveries, and processing backlogs. Staff currently rely on informal messages that often omit critical operational details like carrier name, tracking number, and special handling requirements.

**Pain points addressed:**
- Unstaffed receiving bays due to late or missing shipment notifications
- Informal messages missing critical details — tracking number, handling notes
- No standardised escalation contact if delivery doesn't arrive as scheduled
