## ❗ Problem Being Solved

Without a standardised onboarding brief, new suppliers frequently make avoidable errors — incorrect labelling, missed delivery windows, non-compliant invoicing — creating operational disruption. Manual drafting takes 45 minutes per new supplier. As SwiftFill grows its supplier network, this cost scales linearly without automation.

**Pain points addressed:**
- New supplier errors due to unclear expectations cost time and money to resolve
- 45 minutes manual drafting per onboarding — scales poorly with growth
- Inconsistent information — some suppliers miss key compliance requirements

---

## ⚡ Automation Potential

**Level:** High

| Dimension | Assessment |
|-----------|------------|
| Repetitiveness | Every new supplier — scales directly with business growth |
| Data availability | 90% standardised — only name and category variables change |
| Human judgment needed | Personalisation for specific supplier relationships |
| Integration possibility | Contract management system trigger |
| Estimated time saving | ~90% — from 45 min to ~4 min per onboarding |

**Human-in-the-loop role:** Manager reviews, personalises where needed, and sends. No drafting required.
