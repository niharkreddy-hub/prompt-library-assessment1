## ❗ Problem Being Solved

Without a standardised onboarding brief, new suppliers frequently make avoidable errors — incorrect labelling, missed delivery windows, non-compliant invoicing — creating operational disruption and staff time spent on reactive corrections. Manual drafting takes 45 minutes per new supplier.

**Pain points addressed:**
- New supplier errors due to unclear expectations
- 45 minutes manual drafting per onboarding brief
- Inconsistent information shared — some suppliers miss key requirements
