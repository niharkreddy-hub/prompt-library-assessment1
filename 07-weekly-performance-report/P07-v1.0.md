## ❗ Problem Being Solved

Procurement managers currently compile weekly supplier performance data manually from multiple systems — a process taking 2–3 hours every Monday. Inconsistent reporting makes it difficult to identify at-risk supplier relationships before they cause operational disruption.

**Pain points addressed:**
- 2–3 hours manual Monday reporting across all active suppliers
- Inconsistent format — no week-to-week comparability
- At-risk suppliers not identified until disruption has already occurred
