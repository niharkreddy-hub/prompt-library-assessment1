## 🏢 Intended Workflow or Task

- **Trigger:** WMS inventory falls below reorder threshold
- **Actor:** Procurement coordinator reviews and sends
- **Timing:** Same business day as trigger
- **Next step:** Feeds into P02 if no confirmation in 3 days
```
[WMS reorder alert] → [P01 RUNS] → [Coordinator reviews] → [PO sent to supplier]
```

---

## ❗ Problem Being Solved

Procurement staff spend 35–50 minutes per PO drafting manually. With 20–40 POs issued weekly, this costs SwiftFill up to 2,000 staff minutes per week on a single repetitive task. At $45/hr fully-loaded cost, this represents up to $78,000 annually in administrative labour.

**Pain points addressed:**
- High time cost per PO
- Inconsistent format across staff
- No standardised structure for supplier communication

---

## ⚡ Automation Potential

**Level:** High

| Dimension | Assessment |
|-----------|------------|
| Repetitiveness | 20–40 POs issued per week |
| Data availability | All variables available in WMS |
| Human judgment needed | Review for accuracy before sending |
| Integration possibility | Can connect directly to WMS trigger |
| Estimated time saving | ~75% — from 40 min to ~10 min per PO |

**Human-in-the-loop role:** Coordinator reviews draft and clicks send — no drafting required.

---

## ⚠️ Risks and Limitations

| Risk | Level | Mitigation |
|------|-------|------------|
| Generic output — no structure | High | Need to add RACE role and explicit format |
| Tone too casual for B2B | Medium | No tone instruction in v1.0 |
| Missing required fields | High | No explicit field list provided |

**Overall risk rating:** HIGH — v1.0 output requires significant manual editing before use.

## 🔄 Version History

### v1.0 — Initial draft
**Date:** 31 March 2026  
**Prompt:** You are a procurement coordinator. Write a purchase order email to a supplier requesting stock. Include the order details and delivery date.  
**Output:** Produced a generic, short email with no subject line, no itemised table, casual tone, and missing payment terms and delivery address. Required full manual rewrite.  
**Observed effect:** Estimated 25+ minutes of editing required — no time saving over manual drafting.  
**Lesson learned:** Need RACE role assignment, explicit output structure (subject line, table, sections), formal B2B tone instruction, word limit, and exclusion rules.
