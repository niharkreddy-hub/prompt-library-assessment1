## ❗ Problem Being Solved

Procurement staff spend 35–50 minutes per PO drafting manually. With 20–40 POs issued weekly, this costs SwiftFill up to 2,000 staff minutes per week on a single repetitive task. At $45/hr fully-loaded cost, this represents up to $78,000 annually in administrative labour. Inconsistent formatting also causes supplier confusion and incorrect shipments.

**Pain points addressed:**
- High time cost per PO (35–50 min manual vs target <10 min AI-assisted)
- Inconsistent format across different staff members
- Missing fields cause supplier errors and re-work

## 🔄 Version History

### v1.0 — Initial draft
**Date:** 31 March 2026  
**Prompt:** You are a procurement coordinator. Write a purchase order email to a supplier requesting stock. Include the order details and delivery date.  
**Output:** Generic short email, no subject line, no table, casual tone, missing payment terms and address. Required full manual rewrite.  
**Observed effect:** 25+ minutes editing required — no time saving over manual drafting.  
**Lesson learned:** Need RACE role, explicit structure, formal B2B tone, word limit, and exclusion rules.

---

### v1.1 — Added RACE role + formal tone + placeholder variables
**Date:** 31 March 2026  
**Change:** Added full RACE role assignment ('You are a procurement coordinator at an e-commerce fulfilment company'), 6 explicit placeholder variables, formal B2B tone instruction, and basic output format (subject line, greeting, body, closing).  
**Output:** Significantly improved — professional tone, correct structure, subject line present. Still missing itemised order table and payment terms.  
**Observed effect:** Editing time reduced from 25+ min to approximately 8–10 min. Usable with minor additions.  
**Lesson learned:** RACE role + tone instruction are foundational improvements. Itemised table and payment terms must be explicitly instructed — AI does not infer these from context alone.
